About Jerry Emeseh

Founder and President, Jesus Andreious Army

Jerry Emeseh is a dedicated Christian author and minister passionate about spreading the Gospel and igniting spiritual revival. With a deep commitment to biblical truth, Jerry’s writings focus on calling believers back to God’s ways, addressing moral decline, and promoting global evangelization.

As the founder of Jesus Andreious Army, a non-denominational Christian ministry, Jerry leads efforts to equip the church for impactful ministry. His books and tracts are inspired by divine messages, aiming to transform lives and strengthen the christian faith.

Based in Abuja, Nigeria, Jerry continues to serve through teaching, writing, and partnership initiatives. He has been preaching the Gospel for over 40 years and was called by God into the apostolic ministry—his ministry characterized by signs and wonders to the glory of God.

He is a graduate of Rivers State University of Science and Technology (now Rivers State University) with a degree in Business Administration and is a member of the Chartered Institute of Management of Nigeria.

Jerry is married to Sophia Idachaba, and they are blessed with three children:Runor, Odafe, Fega.
